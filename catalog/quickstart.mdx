## What is Vega Catalog?

Vega Catalog steps in as the perfect companion to Vega DB, akin to Open Metadata (https://open-metadata.org/).
With Vega Catalog, businesses gain a powerful and centralized platform to efficiently organize, document, and govern their data assets.
Seamlessly working together with VegaDB, this integration ensures a seamless flow of metadata, enhancing data lineage, data quality, and fostering a collaborative
data-driven culture. Vega Catalog's user-friendly interface empowers data engineers, analysts, and data scientists to explore, understand, and utilize data
assets to their fullest potential.
